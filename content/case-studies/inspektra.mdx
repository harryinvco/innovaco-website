## The Challenge

Inspektra runs a field service management platform used by inspection and maintenance teams. They had basic analytics in place but lacked the depth needed to make product decisions with confidence. They could see total users and page views, but not which features were being adopted, where users were dropping off, or how usage patterns differed across client accounts.

They needed a full analytics layer built quickly, without re-engineering their application or switching to an expensive enterprise platform.

## What We Built

A 6-dashboard PostHog analytics suite, custom-built for Inspektra's specific product and business metrics.

The six dashboards:
- **Product Overview** — active users, session length, feature usage frequency
- **User Onboarding** — sign-up to first action funnel, drop-off points, time to value
- **Feature Adoption** — per-feature usage rates, trend lines, power user identification
- **Inspection Workflows** — completion rates, time per inspection, field vs. office usage
- **Client Health** — per-account usage patterns, engagement scoring, churn indicators
- **Business Metrics** — MRR correlation, support ticket volume vs. feature usage, NPS drivers

Each dashboard was built with Inspektra's team to ensure the metrics tracked matched the decisions they actually needed to make. We implemented custom event tracking across key user actions and built the PostHog queries to surface actionable insights rather than vanity metrics.

## The Result

The dashboards are now central to Inspektra's weekly product meetings. Feature prioritisation is driven by adoption data. Onboarding improvements are measured against actual funnel metrics. Client health scores give early warning on accounts that need attention. The entire suite was delivered at €5,600 and continues to run without additional cost.
