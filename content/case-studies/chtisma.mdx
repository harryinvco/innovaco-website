## The Challenge

Chtisma Trading operates in the competitive Cyprus construction materials market. Their sales team handled a high volume of product enquiries — availability, specifications, pricing, and delivery timelines. Internally, staff needed quick access to a large product catalogue and frequently updated pricing without digging through spreadsheets.

Both problems needed solving simultaneously. The customer experience and the internal workflow were two sides of the same information gap.

## What We Built

Two AI chatbots running on a shared Dify infrastructure, self-hosted on EU servers.

The customer-facing bot was trained on Chtisma's full product catalogue, pricing tiers, and delivery information. It handles enquiries in Greek and English and can guide buyers toward the right product category based on their project needs.

The internal staff bot was built for the sales team. It provides instant access to current pricing, product specifications, stock status, and cross-referencing between product lines. Sales staff use it during calls and at the counter to answer questions faster.

Both bots were demonstrated live at a Cyprus construction industry gala event, where Chtisma showcased how they were using AI to improve service quality in a traditionally manual sector.

## The Result

Chtisma now runs AI on both sides of their business. Customers get instant answers outside business hours. Staff spend less time looking up information and more time closing sales. The gala demonstration positioned Chtisma as a forward-thinking company in the Cyprus construction industry.
