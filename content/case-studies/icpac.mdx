## The Challenge

ICPAC manages thousands of member enquiries annually — CPD requirements, regulatory updates, exam information, and membership administration. Staff were spending significant time answering the same questions repeatedly, in both Greek and English, across email, phone, and in person.

## What We Built

A bilingual RAG-powered chatbot deployed on ICPAC's own subdomain, built on Dify and self-hosted on Hetzner EU infrastructure. The knowledge base was built from ICPAC's existing documentation — regulations, CPD requirements, exam syllabi, membership FAQs, and procedural guides.

The bot handles Greek and English within the same conversation, switching language based on how the member writes — not a dropdown selection.

Key capabilities:
- CPD hour queries and deadline information
- Exam registration and syllabus guidance
- Membership tier and subscription questions
- Regulatory update summaries
- Escalation to human staff for complex queries

## The Result

The chatbot handles the majority of routine member enquiries without staff involvement. Staff time is redirected to complex queries and member services that genuinely require human judgment. All member data stays on ICPAC's EU-hosted infrastructure — no data touches US servers.
